Who We Are

About MIYETU Tree Foundation

MIYETU Tree Foundation is a grassroots environmental organization based in Kawangware, Nairobi, Kenya. We are dedicated to creating a sustainable future through environmental conservation, tree planting initiatives, and climate change education.

Our Mission

To promote environmental sustainability through tree planting, climate change education, and advocating for the conservation of the environment.

Our Vision

To create a green, sustainable environment and a better home for all living things.

Our Motto

Together we can make a difference.

Our Core Objectives

01

Raise awareness on environmental conservation

02

Encourage and facilitate tree planting initiatives

03

Educate communities on climate change mitigation and adaptation

04

Collaborate with government agencies and stakeholders

05

Conduct research on environmental conservation

Tree Planting Program
